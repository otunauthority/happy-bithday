Act Three — The Letter

My Favour,

Happy birthday to the best part of my everyday.

If I had a million dollars, I'd spend all of it trying to build you a celebration big enough to match how much you mean to me — and I still wouldn't come close. So instead, I built you this. Every spark, every balloon, every silly effect on this page is just my clumsy way of saying: I am so grateful you exist, and even more grateful that you're mine.

You have this way of walking into a room, or a bad day, and making it lighter just by being in it. You worry sometimes, more than you need to, about things that haven't even happened yet. And every single time, I tell you the same three words, because they're the truest thing I know:

You will be fine.

Not because I'm just saying it to calm you down — but because I've watched you handle everything life has thrown at you with more strength than you ever give yourself credit for. You will be fine, today, this year, and every year after, because you are the kind of person who makes things fine just by showing up.

So today, do less worrying and more celebrating. Let yourself be spoiled a little. You deserve every candle, every confetti burst, and every kind word coming your way — and from me, always.

Happy birthday, my love. Here's to another year of us.

Forever yours.
